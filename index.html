Baymax is your personal mental health assistant

Baymax


Welcome to Baymax, a personal mental health assistant whose job is to make sure that you are being the best you possible. How did we come up with this idea? We were introduced to many insightful and impactful companies through live demonstrations and their amazing APIs. Afterwards, we decided to integrate Google Cloud Platform's API, Twilio's API, and Alexa's API in order to complete our project. The amazing thing about Baymax is that it is able to detect and formulate predictions about a user's mental health based off of his or her responses to a question.
